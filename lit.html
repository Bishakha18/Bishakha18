My Poetry


Jindagi Aati Hai Nani Kaliyon Ki Tarah, Aur Chali Jaati Hai Ret ki tarah...
Thahar Jaati Hai Kabhi Kisi Mod Par, Aur badhati Chali Jaati Hai Rahon Ki Tarah..
Koi Katata Hai ise Mujrim Ki Tarah, To Koi Jeeta is Mohabbat Ki Tarah..
Jindagi Hai yah Mitti ki Sondhi Khushboo se , Par fisal Jaati Hai Ret si...
Hai ye baraf Ki Mithi thandak Si, per Deti Hai Gam dhup ki chubti Garmi Si..
Lagti Hai Northern Plains ke level si, Per Hoti Hai mountains ki chadhai si..
Zindagi Ek Nayi ummid hai, hai ye nahin Tarang..
Aasaon ke Pankh khol de, hai ye udati Patang..
Banati Hai buniyad Jiski,hai ye nahin chadhan..
Girati hai Guman jismein, Hai yah iski dhang..
Na pa sake isko Dobara, To Jee le Iske Har Rang...
